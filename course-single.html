About Course

For Grades 8, 9 and 10
What are foundation courses?
Foundation courses for classes 8, 9 and 10 are conducted in order to build a strong foundation of the basic concepts in the subjects like Science and Mathematics. These courses train for school examinations and simultaneously train you for various other competitive examinations. Our foundation courses for the grade 8, 9 and 10 mould students to develop out of the cave thinking and take them beyond the confines of school books to install in them a methodical scientific temperament and inculcate the love for subjects. The courses aim at developing conceptual clarity, strong grasp in the curriculum and exceptional performance in various competitive exams including National and International Olympiads, National Talent Search Examinations (NTSE), State Talent Search Examinations (STSE), KVPY etc.
